Shannon Ehmsen

Graphics Assistant II, 4D Nucleome DCIC

Shannon Ehmsen is a Graphics Designer / Assistant with the 4D Nucleome Data Coordination and Integration Center and Park Lab's Clinical Genome Analysis Platform (CGAP). She recently graduated with a Bachelor in Fine Arts in Studio Art from Northeastern University and the School of the Museum of Fine Arts at Tufts University in 2019 and started at the Park Lab soon after.
