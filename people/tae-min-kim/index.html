Dr. Tae-min Kim

MD in Medicine and PhD in Microbiology, The Catholic University of Korea, Postdoctoral Fellow (2008-2012), Professor, Department of Medical Informatics, The Catholic University of Korea, Seoul, Korea

Tae-Min received his MD/PhD from The Catholic University of Korea in 2008. He worked on analysis of DNA copy number in tumor genomes as well as several other collaborations. He runs his own lab at his alma mater.
