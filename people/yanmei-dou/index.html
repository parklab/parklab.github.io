Dr. Yanmei Dou

PhD in Bioinformatics, Peking University (China), Postdoctoral Fellow (2017-2021), Principal Investigator, Westlake University

Yanmei completed her PhD in Bioinformatics at Peking University. She co-developed MosaicHunter, a Bayesian-based software to identify post-zygotic mosaicisms in whole-genome sequencing and whole-exome sequencing of non-cancer individuals using unpaired, paired, or trio samples (http://mosaichunter.cbi.pku.edu.cn/), and performed bioinformatic analysis of single nucleotide mosaicisms in exome data of ASD pedigrees. She is interested in somatic mutations in human genome.
